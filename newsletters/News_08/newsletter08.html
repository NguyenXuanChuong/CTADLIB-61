WHAT’S IN A ®??

You see them on every
letterhead and flyer.
Trademarks (™) and Service
Marks (SM) are used by
companies to indicate to the
public that a particular brand
is unique and protected.
Registration of a trademark
(®) is a higher level of
recognition, of which the
highest is a registration on
the Principal Register. The
United States Patent and
Trademark Office performs
due diligence to see if a
trademarked phrase or logo
conflicts with other brands to
determine when they are
genuinely unique, prior to
awarding a Registered
Trademark ®.

Several of our affiliated
companies hold registered
trademarks, the first of which
was awarded over 20 years
ago to Mind Over Body®.
Our latest officially registered
trademarks are
Comprehensive Network®,
Comprehensive Resources®,
Comprehensive Enrichment®,
Be A School Nurse!®, and
Binet®! Soon, we will also
own a trademark for
Comprehensive Advantage™.


*The highest form of registration
by the US Patent &
Trademark Office is a registration
on the Principal Register –
assuring that we, our marks and
brand are unique, without any
qualification, and protected.
