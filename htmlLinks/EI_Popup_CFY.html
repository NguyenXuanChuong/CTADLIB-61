Clinical Fellowship Year Program

Netcare, Inc. is a leading NYC agency providing a variety of services to eligible children since 1998.  Join our energetic and knowledgeable program as a Clinical Fellow (CF/SLP). Develop your clinical skills and reach your professional goals!

  • Work in diverse and rewarding settings - home, school, daycare, community
  • Gain experience providing speech, language, feeding and oral-motor therapy for children ages birth - five. 
  • Increase your knowledge and skills working with a dynamic population -developmental delays, Autism/PDD, Apraxia, Dysarthria, feeding issues
