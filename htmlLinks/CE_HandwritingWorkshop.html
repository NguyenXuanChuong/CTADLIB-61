Jan's workshops are FUN, creative, and multi-sensory and are focused on empowering therapists and teachers to make instruction and therapy more successful for children who struggle with handwriting. This NEW workshop for special populations is geared to instruction of special needs children, including children with autism, learning disabilities, and dysgraphia. Attendees will participate in multi-sensory strategies through lab activities. Creative solutions for grasp development, fine motor skills, and visual motor cueing will be discussed, and extensive handouts and resources on a CD will be provided. Participants will also develop their own tool kit of therapeutic strategies for fine motor, visual motor and handwriting skills! This is a workshop that even seasoned therapists and teachers will find inspiring.

Wear comfortable clothing, because paint and messy activities are part of the workshop fun!

All attendees will receive a workshop CD with an abundant amount
of resources, handouts, developmental checklists, and adapted curriculum.
