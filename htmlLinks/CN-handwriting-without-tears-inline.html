This workshop provides dynamic instruction in the Handwriting Without Tears (HWT) program, covering preschool through cursive writing. The HWT methodology incorporates engaging multi sensory techniques and research-based methods to make handwriting a natural and automatic skill for children of all ages and abilities. Workshop presenter Bonnie Shams, OTR/L, is a sensory motor specialist who has used the HWT curriculum in schools for more than 15 years, through her affiliation with Comprehensive Network and in her private practice.
