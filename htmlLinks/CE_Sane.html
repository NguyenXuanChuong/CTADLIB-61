SEMINAR DESCRIPTION:  
What’s the difference between success and frustration in treatment?
S.A.N.E. (Sleep, Activities, Nutrition and Environment)

This course combines understanding of the sensory motor and biochemical connections that impact developmental disorders and behaviors in children from a neurological perspective and provides tools necessary to prioritize treatment and measure outcomes.

It will address the child - birth through teen - who is demonstrating delays, including PDD, Autism, LD, failure to thrive, depression, hyperactivity, sensory processing and language disorders and provide a variety of successful treatment strategies utilizing the S.A.N.E. approach.

Course is designed to challenge physical, occupational and speech pathologists. Participants will receive a comprehensive companion manual and references to the clinical presentation and video case studies.

“These have been termed SANE strategies because they WORK. When all four aspects of a child's functioning are considered in relation to each other and combined with all clinical assessments, we have the tools for a truly successful plan. Our goal is to help each child to function and to feel their very best.” – Anne Buckley-Reen, Debra Dickson

INSTRUCTOR:
Debra Dickson, RPT, is a renowned clinician and owner of TherapyWorks Pediatrics, LLC in Old Lyme, CT. She is NDT certified in pediatrics, has been a pediatric therapist for 28 years and has a strong background in sensory processing, nutrition and immune function. Debra and Anne Buckley-Reen, OTR, developed this program for therapists and are currently co-authoring a companion manual for parents by the same title. She works closely with behavioral optometrists in treating the Visual/Vestibular system and teaches a two day course for therapists entitled “Visual-Vestibular Assessment and Treatment to Improve Sensory Processing, Motor Skills, Learning and Behavior.” Debra lectures both locally and nationally on these topics and sits on the professional advisory committees of several nonprofit agencies.
