Families as Partners

  • Get Involved!  Be part of the process, have fun, give you child praise for trying new activities.
  • Make Every Minute Count!  When your child’s therapists works with your child, observes and try to activities that they recommend.  Ask questions and offer feedback on how your child is doing.
  • Ask for Help!  Let your therapist know if you or your child are struggling with their suggestions or activities.  Communication is the key!
  • Enjoy the Everyday!  There are ways of incorporating developmental skills into everything you do.  Your therapist can help you learn ways of helping your child develop while you carry out your daily routine.
  • Use the Tools!  The FAP calendar is an excellent tool for monitoring progress and communication with your therapist.
  • Provide Variety!  Change the activities you do with your child and provide them with new and interesting views of their environment to keep them motivated to grow and develop.
  • Use your Support System!  Involve friends, family and other caretakers in helping your child meet their developmental outcomes.
